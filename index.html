About Us

JR Engineering is a leading provider of comprehensive electrical engineering solutions. Our expertise encompasses:

  • Supply and installation of high and medium voltage equipment, including transformers, generators, and circuit breakers
  • Specialized services for motors, capacitors, and various cable types
  • Installation of electrical infrastructure components such as panel boards, light fittings, and earthing systems
  • Professional electrical design, drawing preparation, and approval processes
  • Rigorous inspection, testing, and maintenance services for critical electrical systems
  • Expert handling of electrical breakdowns and emergency repairs
  • Specialized maintenance for transformers, circuit breakers, and protection relays

With our extensive industry knowledge and commitment to excellence, we deliver reliable, efficient, and innovative electrical solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients across various sectors.
